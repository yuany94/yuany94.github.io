Yuan Yuan

Assistant Professor, Purdue University

Hi! My name is Yuan Yuan (袁源). I am a computational social scientist and an assistant professor at the Krannert School of Management (MIS area) at Purdue University.

I am interested in

  • leveraging big data and advanced computational techniques (e.g., machine learning and causal inference) to study social and organizational networks.
  • developing computational techniques that combine machine learning and causal inference, with applications to online field experiments (A/B testing).

Before Purdue, I did my PhD in Institute for Data, Systems, and Society (IDSS) at Massachusetts Institute of Technology. I received my Bachelor's degrees with honors in Computer Science and Economics from Tsinghua University.

In AY 2022-2023, I am visiting Microsoft Office of Applied Research (part-time). My research involves close industry collaborations, including Microsoft, Meta, and Tencent/WeChat.
